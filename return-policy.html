All Yash12007 products are digitally delivered. Due to the nature of digital content, we typically do not accept returns or offer refunds after the product has been delivered, activated, or installed.

We may consider returns under the following circumstances:

Return Timeframe

Return requests must be submitted within 7 days of purchase. Requests made after this period are generally not eligible for refund.
