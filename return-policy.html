Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Thank you for choosing Yash12007. We are committed to delivering high-quality digital software and applications. While our products are digitally delivered, we do accept return and refund requests for both defective and non-defective products, subject to the conditions outlined below.

Eligibility for Returns

A return or refund request may be approved only if a valid and justifiable reason is provided. All requests are carefully reviewed to ensure fair usage and prevent misuse.

Valid Reasons for Return

Returns may be accepted under the following conditions:

  • The software is defective, not functioning, or fails to install on supported systems.
  • The product does not match the description provided.
  • Technical issues that cannot be resolved by our support team.
  • Accidental duplicate purchase or billing errors.
  • Non-defective returns with a valid explanation (subject to approval).

Non-Defective Product Returns

Returns for non-defective products are not automatically approved. Requests must include a clear and reasonable explanation, and approval depends on internal review.

Returns will NOT be accepted if:

  • The product has already been fully used or consumed for its intended purpose.
  • The request is made without a valid or specific reason.
  • The customer changes their mind without justification.
  • The product functions as described and meets expectations outlined in documentation.

Return Request Timeframe

All return or refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of purchase. Requests submitted after this period may not be eligible.

Review & Approval Process

Each request is manually reviewed. To process your request, please provide:

  • Your Order ID or transaction reference
  • A detailed reason for the return
  • Proof of purchase
  • Screenshots or video evidence (if applicable)

Our team reserves the right to approve or reject any request based on the information provided.

Fair Usage Policy

To maintain fairness, we enforce a strict policy against misuse. Returns may be denied if we detect patterns such as repeated purchase-and-return behaviour or attempts to exploit the system.

Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 3–7 business days.

How to Request a Return

To request a return or refund, please contact us via email and include all required details.
