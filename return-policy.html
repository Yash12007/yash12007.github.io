Thank you for choosing Yash12007. Our goal is to provide high-quality software and applications that meet or exceed user expectations. Please review our return policy carefully before requesting a refund.

Digital Goods Return Policy

All Yash12007 products are digitally delivered software or applications. Due to the nature of digital products, returns or refunds are generally not provided after the product has been delivered, activated, or installed.

Defective Product Returns

A return or refund may be approved if the purchased software is defective or not functioning as intended.

A product may be considered defective if:

  • The software fails to install or run correctly on supported systems.
  • The product does not function as described in official documentation.
  • Activation or licensing fails despite a valid purchase.
  • Critical features do not work and the issue cannot be resolved by our support team.

In such cases, customers may be required to provide evidence of the defect, such as screenshots, logs, or videos demonstrating the problem.

Exceptions to the No-Refund Policy

Refunds may also be considered under the following circumstances:

  • The product is defective and support is unable to resolve the issue within 7 days.
  • You were charged more than once due to a billing or technical error.
  • The software was not delivered or activation failed after successful payment.

Return Request Timeframe

Return or refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of purchase. Requests submitted after this period may not be eligible for a refund.

How to Request a Return

To request a return or refund, please contact us via email and include the following information:

  • Your Order ID or transaction reference
  • A detailed description of the issue
  • Proof of purchase
  • Screenshots or videos showing the problem (if applicable)
